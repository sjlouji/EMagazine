Department

Every moment we experience Information Technology around us in one or other form. Department was started in the year 2010 with B. Tech. IT program with a sanctioned intake of 60 students. The Department has over 13 faculty members with 2 PhD holders and 2 faculty who are pursuing their PhD. The Department offers a programme that combines classroom and laboratory learning in technical areas with liberal arts and cooperative work assignments. This creates a practical program, balanced between foundation studies in the engineering science and applications in the real world of work. The students are provided with all facilities and guidance to carry out their project work in the department. Intensive laboratory assignments are given to significant experience with the state of the art facilities and design tools. The Department emphasizes on Domain Specific Applications such as : Networks, Information Security, Software Engineering, Operating Systems, Database Management Systems, Image Processing, Cloud Computing etc. to facilitate the students in acquiring higher degree of knowledge, global competency and excellence, for the betterment of the society.
